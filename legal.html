PRACTICES

Navigating corporate governance, compliance, and strategic decisions requires a trusted legal advisor. TFL’s corporate law team is equipped to help businesses of all sizes operate smoothly and efficiently, from inception to growth. We assist with:
  • Company Formation & Structuring
  • Convertibles Notes & SAFEs
  • Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A)
  • Shareholder & Partnership Agreements
  • Capital Raising & Private Equity
  • Corporate Restructuring & Joint Ventures
We provide pragmatic advice that ensures your corporate strategy is not only legally sound but also aligned with your broader business objectives.

Our commercial law services cover every aspect of business transactions. TFL helps businesses negotiate, draft, and enforce contracts that safeguard your interests. Whether you are entering into new commercial relationships or expanding existing ones, we provide expert guidance on:
  • Contract Drafting, Negotiation & Review
  • Convertible Notes and SAFEs
  • Supply & Distribution Agreements
  • Licensing, IP, and Technology Agreements
  • E-Commerce, B2B, and Commercial Transactions
  • Terms & Conditions, Service Level Agreements (SLAs)
  • Franchising
Our team works to ensure that your commercial contracts are robust, clear, and designed to protect your long-term business interests.

Employment law is critical to the success and compliance of your workforce. Whether you're an employer managing employee relations or an employee seeking advice, TFL can assist with employment matters, including:
  • Employment Contracts & Policies
  • Workplace Disputes Resolution & Mediation
  • Termination Process
  • Labor Compliance & Employment Regulations
  • Employee Benefits & Compensation (ESOP)
  • Employment Immigration Matters
We provide practical legal solutions that help businesses stay compliant and foster positive working relationships within the organization.

At TFL, our intention is to deter your exposure to courts. We offer tailored Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) services to help you resolve disputes quickly, efficiently, and without the need for lengthy litigation. We provide expert arbitration and mediation services in both DIFC and ADGM jurisdictions, ensuring that your business remains protected and that disputes are handled in a professional and confidential manner.
  • Arbitration: We represent clients in domestic and international arbitrations, ensuring that the process is efficient and the outcome favorable.
  • Mediation: Our mediation services aim to facilitate settlement through negotiation, offering a less formal and more collaborative way to resolve disputes.
  • Dispute Resolution Strategy: Tailored solutions designed to avoid escalation, reduce costs, and ensure that your business interests are protected at every step.
We specialize in both DIFC and ADGM arbitration and mediation services, ensuring that your business disputes are managed under internationally recognized and respected legal frameworks.
